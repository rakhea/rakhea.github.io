About Me

I'm an Instructional Designer at Blackbaud, Hyderabad — with over five years of experience spanning SaaS product training, blended learning delivery, quality operations, and HR systems at scale.

My work sits at the intersection of pedagogy, design, and technology. I'm drawn to the challenge of making complex SaaS products feel learnable — through clear structure, real-world scenarios, and experiences that respect the learner's time.

I bring a creative lens to everything I do: from gamified compliance courses that actually get completed, to AI-assisted rapid prototyping that cuts development cycles in half. Currently targeting Deputy Manager / Assistant Manager level L&D roles where I can lead programs and mentor teams — not just build content.

Outside of work, I'm deeply interested in the frontier of generative AI in learning design, visual communication, and building systems that scale.
